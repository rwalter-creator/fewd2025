At Bean Boutique, we believe that every cup tells a story.

Nestled in the heart of Port of Spain, located opposite the St. Ann's Cascade Taxi Stand, our coffee shop is more than just a place to grab your daily caffeine fix. It’s a sanctuary for coffee lovers, a hub for creativity, and a warm embrace for anyone who steps through our doors.

Our journey begins with ethically sourced beans from the finest farms around the globe, handpicked for their rich flavors and bold aromas. From farm to cup, we take pride in every step of the process.

Are you a new customer to Bean Boutique?

Crafted with Passion, Served with Love

Our beans are sourced from the finest coffee farms around the world, ensuring rich flavors and aromatic perfection in every cup. From bold espressos to creamy lattes, each beverage is crafted by our expert baristas with precision and care.

A Space to Savor and Socialize

With warm lighting, stylish decor, and a relaxed atmosphere, Bean Boutique is the perfect spot to sip, savor, and connect. Whether you're meeting friends, catching up on work, or simply enjoying a quiet moment, our inviting ambiance makes every visit special.

More Than Just Coffee

Pair your favorite coffee with our delectable pastries, artisanal sandwiches, and wholesome treats, all made fresh daily. We also offer dairy-free, gluten-free, and vegan options to suit every preference.

Come Visit Us!

We’re excited to welcome you to Bean Boutique—where every cup tells a story. Stop by today and indulge in the art of exceptional coffee.
